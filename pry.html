Privacy Policy for Lena Dena BankIntroductionWelcome to Lena Dena Bank's Privacy Policy. At Lena Dena Bank, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring that your personal and financial information is handled securely and responsibly. CollectWe may collect the following types of personal and financial information from you:Personal Information: Name, address, date of birth, contact details, identification documents, etc.Financial Information: Bank account details, credit history, income, employment details, etc.Transactional Information: Details of transactions, loan applications, payments, etc.Technical Information: IP address, browser type, device information, cookies, etc. (collected automatically when you visit our website or use our mobile banking app).How We Use Your InformationWe use the information we collect obligations, regulatory requirements, and law enforcement agencies as required by law.Business Partners: With your consent, we may share your information with our business partners for marketing and promotional purposes.We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties for their marketing purposes.Security and Data ProtectionWe implement robust security measures and encryption protocols to protect your personal and financial information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction.
